Frequently asked questions.

What is 10Web?

10Web is an AI-powered platform designed to simplify the process of building, hosting, and managing WordPress websites. We offer tools like an AI Website Builder, automated hosting on Google Cloud, PageSpeed Booster, a variety of AI tools to kickstart your business, and a unified dashboard for website management.

How does 10Web's AI Website Builder work?

The AI Website Builder allows users to create a fully functional website by providing a brief description of their business. The AI generates layouts, sets design preferences, and builds the site in minutes. Users can further customize the site using a drag-and-drop editor enhanced with AI Co-Pilot.

Can I create an ecommerce website with 10Web?

Yes, our platform enables users to create ecommerce websites with ease by simply providing their store description. The AI Ecommerce Website Builder integrates WooCommerce automatically, providing full functionality for online stores. Users can manage products, orders, and payments through the 10Web dashboard.
